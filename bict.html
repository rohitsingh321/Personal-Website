Overview of BICT Program

The Bachelor in Information and Communication Technology (BICT) is a four-year program designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the field of information technology and communication. The program covers a wide range of subjects including programming, networking, data science, and more.

BICT Course Structure

  • First Year: Introduction to Programming, Mathematics, Basic Electronics, and more.
  • Second Year: Data Structures, Object-Oriented Programming, Database Systems, and more.
  • Third Year: Operating Systems, Networking, Web Development, and more.
  • Fourth Year: Artificial Intelligence, Mobile Computing, Project Work, and more.

BICT Syllabus - Semester Wise

First Semester

  • Course 1: Introduction to Programming
  • Course 2: Mathematics I
  • Course 3: Basic Electronics

Second Semester

  • Course 1: Data Structures
  • Course 2: Mathematics II
  • Course 3: Object-Oriented Programming
